Preview reviewers must evaluate:

- Does the homepage read as a matchday operating system?
- Are all four lanes obvious above the fold or immediately visible on mobile?
- Is inquiry the primary path?
- Is VIP presented as premium planning and review, not a ride product?
- Is Tahoe controlled as subject to confirmation?
- Is Team Family privacy protected?
- Is Media / Corporate quote-ready without overclaiming fleet or dispatch?
- Does Fan Hub scale through guidance and activation without promising matching?
- Are ad/referral zones restricted to public fan contexts?
- Are command hub states separated from confirmed service?
- Is the QR destination clear and safe?
- Are all claims visibly bounded?
