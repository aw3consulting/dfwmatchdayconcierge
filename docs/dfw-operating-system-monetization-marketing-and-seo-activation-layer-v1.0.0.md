# DFW Operating System Monetization, Marketing, and SEO Activation Layer v1.0.0

## 1. Purpose

This file closes the audit gap for monetization, marketing, acquisition, ad placement, and SEO.

## 2. Monetization Architecture

### VIP Guest Concierge

Allowed monetization:

- activation fee
- private planning fee
- daily, weekly, full-stay, or approved block quote
- Black Executive Vehicle Class review
- food, cooler, luggage, comfort, security coordination, and special requests by quote
- deposit / hold fee after operator review

No automated payment claim is approved.

### Team Family Support

Allowed monetization:

- private support activation
- representative/coordinator support fee
- family itinerary planning fee
- full-stay or multi-day quote
- add-on coordination by quote

Ad placement is prohibited unless explicitly approved.

### Media / Corporate

Allowed monetization:

- quote-based group logistics package
- recurring schedule fee
- executive movement coordination
- crew/equipment/luggage coordination
- catering/hospitality coordination
- company-card or invoice path by review

### Team Fan Hubs

Allowed monetization:

- free public guidance
- activated planning upsell
- local referral paths
- affiliate links by disclosure
- sponsored public fan placements
- public ad-supported route, restaurant, attraction, and watch-planning content

### Drivers

Driver lane is a capacity lane, not a guaranteed work or earnings product.

No employment, contractor, or earnings claim may publish before terms are approved.

### Concierges

Concierge lane is a capacity/support lane, not a public compensation product.

No role authority or guaranteed work claim may publish before terms are approved.

### Fulfillment

Allowed monetization:

- direct service margin where capacity exists
- coordination fee
- referral fee
- affiliate/API revenue where disclosed
- information-only and restricted path separation

## 3. Ad Placement Rules

Allowed ad/referral zones:

- public Fan Hubs
- public route guides
- public attraction guides
- food and nightlife guides
- hotel area guidance
- public team hub content

Restricted zones:

- VIP hubs
- Team Family hubs
- private Media / Corporate hubs
- paid command hubs
- driver command hubs
- concierge command hubs

Required labels:

- sponsored
- affiliate
- referral
- third-party
- information only
- operator-assisted

## 4. Marketing and Acquisition Minimum

Required launch messages:

- coordinator pitch
- VIP pitch
- Media / Corporate pitch
- Fan Hub pitch
- driver recruiting pitch
- concierge recruiting pitch
- first response script
- QR/business-card field-use plan

Initial response promise:

`Send your lane, dates, locations, group size, and matchday needs. We will review and respond with next steps.`

## 5. SEO Minimum

Production candidate requires:

- title tags by route
- meta descriptions by route
- canonical URLs
- Open Graph tags
- favicon/social preview
- Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, DFW Airport, Love Field keyword coverage
- matchday concierge language
- media/corporate language
- driver onboarding language
- fan hub language
- sitemap/robots decision
- preview indexing control
