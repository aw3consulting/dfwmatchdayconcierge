## 8. Global Blocked Language and Behaviors

The following remain blocked from final copy until separately approved:

1. Official FIFA, venue, airport, hotel, team, government, sponsor, or partner affiliation claims.
2. Guaranteed 24/7 fulfillment claims.
3. Guaranteed ride, booking, dispatch, airport, luggage, hospitality, or VIP service availability claims.
4. Insured, licensed, certified, permitted, official, approved, or verified claims without evidence.
5. Confirmed title sponsor or sponsor rail partner claims.
6. Traffic, audience, ad inventory, conversion, or paid placement performance claims.
7. Automated booking, dispatch, AI intake, or QR activation claims that exceed current operator-assisted workflow.
8. Final-domain production readiness language.
9. QR activation language.
10. DreamHost deployment readiness language.
11. Batch 1 progression language.
12. Final public contact/action language before contact-flow review.

## 9. Sponsor-Safety Rules

1. Title Sponsor and Sponsor Rail may be planned as surfaces only.
2. Sponsor surfaces may not name sponsors.
3. Sponsor surfaces may not show logos.
4. Sponsor surfaces may not guarantee availability, exposure, impressions, traffic, reach, conversion, exclusivity, or placement performance.
5. Sponsor surfaces may not imply a paid placement already exists.
6. Sponsor language must remain subordinate to visitor trust and service clarity.
7. Sponsor copy insertion requires a later sponsor/monetization review and explicit user approval.

## 10. Independent-Platform Clarity Rules

Section 3 and Footer must preserve independent-platform clarity.

Allowed planning categories:

1. Independent service posture.
2. Sponsor disclosure category.
3. Responsible planning category.
4. Capability scope category.
5. Source distinction category.

Blocked unless separately approved:

1. Overbroad legal disclaimers.
2. Official affiliation denials that create new claims.
3. Insurance, licensing, certification, authorization, approval, or endorsement claims without evidence.
4. Claims that imply public agency, team, venue, FIFA, airport, hotel, or sponsor approval.
