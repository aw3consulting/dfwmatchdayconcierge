# DFW Command Hub Commercial Tier and Activation Model v1.0.0

## 1. Purpose

This file governs the commercial tier structure and activation model for DFW Matchday Concierge Command Hubs. It defines how public, free, verified, activated, VIP, Team Family, Media / Corporate, and Fan hub experiences differ, how each may monetize, where advertising is allowed, where planning deposits apply, where operator approval applies, and where fulfillment remains optional.

This layer resolves the most important unresolved commercial determination created by the monetization gain/loss and non-fulfillment governance layer.

## 2. Core Determination

DFW Matchday Concierge must monetize the information and planning layer independently from direct fulfillment.

Direct fulfillment remains valuable but capacity-constrained. The scalable commercial asset is the Command Hub system.

The hub model must support:

- free public information
- verified inquiry continuation
- activated personal planning
- VIP / Team Family operator-approved activation
- Media / Corporate activation
- Fan hub monetization through planning deposit, ads, referrals, affiliates, and provider routing
- fulfillment only when directly accepted or coordinated

## 3. Command Hub Tier Stack

The governed commercial tier stack is:

1. Public Matchday Hub
2. Free Fan / Team Hub
3. Verified Matchday Hub Preview
4. Activated Personal Matchday Command Hub
5. VIP Guest Concierge Hub
6. Team Family Support Hub
7. Media / Corporate Command Hub
8. Operator / Admin Command Layer

## 4. Tier 1: Public Matchday Hub

Purpose:
Traffic capture, trust building, orientation, and inquiry conversion.

Visible to:

- all visitors

May include:

- DFW overview
- World Cup matchday orientation
- public service lane cards
- airport/stadium/area guidance
- team hub entry
- fan experience guidance
- public concierge inquiry CTA
- public referral/affiliate links if approved
- public ads if approved

Monetization:

- ads
- affiliate/referral links
- inquiry conversion
- lead capture
- public sponsorship

No guarantee of:

- direct fulfillment
- booking acceptance
- private itinerary
- driver assignment
- VIP eligibility

## 5. Tier 2: Free Fan / Team Hub

Purpose:
Serve high-volume fan traffic without consuming operator capacity.

Visible to:

- team fans
- public visitors
- international supporters
- non-paying users

May include:

- team-specific public guidance
- match schedule
- advancement watch if data is available
- food and attraction recommendations
- route guidance
- Fan Experience planning
- public shared ride interest CTA
- referral links
- affiliate links
- controlled ad widgets

Monetization:

- ads
- affiliate/referral links
- sponsored placements
- upgraded activated hub conversion
- shared ride inquiry conversion

Ads:

Allowed when controlled and useful.

No guarantee of:

- personalized planning
- custom itinerary
- driver availability
- shared ride match
- VIP/Tahoe access

## 6. Tier 3: Verified Matchday Hub Preview

Purpose:
Convert serious users from public browsing into structured inquiry and planning.

Access requirement:

- email and/or mobile verification
- secure link or authenticated session

May include:

- AI-assisted intake summary
- preliminary trip plan
- missing information checklist
- recommended widgets
- next-step activation CTA
- operator review pending status

Monetization:

- conversion to activated hub
- conversion to planning deposit
- conversion to direct fulfillment inquiry
- data-qualified referral/affiliate routing

Ads:

Allowed only for fan/non-premium preview states. Not recommended for VIP, Team Family, Media, or Corporate previews.

No guarantee of:

- final itinerary
- pricing
- direct fulfillment
- driver assignment
- payment acceptance

Activation methods:

- Concierge Planning Deposit
- Command Hub Activation
- operator approval

- Team Family/VIP privacy approval
- manual operator override

May include:

- detailed itinerary builder
- activated widgets
- guest details
- payment status
- quote status
- service requests
- personalized recommendations
- referral/provider routing
- operator review status

Monetization:

- planning deposit
- package conversion
- referral/affiliate conversion
- direct fulfillment conversion
- add-on sales

Ads:

Not recommended except for approved fan-tier activated hubs.

No guarantee of:

- service confirmation until operator acceptance and payment terms are complete

## 8. Tier 5: VIP Guest Concierge Hub

Purpose:
Manage high-value, privacy-sensitive, multi-day, weekly, or full-stay VIP planning.

Activation methods:

- operator-approved activation
- planning deposit if appropriate
- direct VIP acceptance
- coordinator/representative authority approval

May include:

- VIP itinerary
- Tahoe eligibility/assignment status
- VIP driver/operator status
- privacy-sensitive planning
- food/beverage/cooler requests
- luggage support
- security coordination request
- budget and quote review
- guest or assistant coordination

Monetization:

- high-value concierge package
- multi-day/weekly/full-stay service
- planning deposit where appropriate
- premium add-ons
- direct fulfillment
- selective premium referrals

Ads:

Prohibited unless expressly approved as curated premium placement.

No guarantee of:

- Tahoe availability until assigned
- driver availability until confirmed
- security until coordinated and accepted
- official access

No guarantee of:

- payment contact status
- quote and invoice status

Monetization:

- corporate planning

## 11. Tier 8: Operator / Admin Command Layer

Purpose:
Internal review, scoring, allocation, pricing, confirmation, driver assignment, hub activation, and fulfillment control.

Visible to:

- authorized DFW Matchday Concierge operator/admin only

May include:

- scoring engine
- privacy flags
- capacity review
- Tahoe allocation
- driver pool status
- payment status
- quote status
- referral routing
- fulfillment decision
- decline/restrict logic

Monetization role:

- prevents free over-servicing
- protects VIP capacity
- routes non-fulfillable demand to monetized alternatives
- controls quote and confirmation

## 12. Activation Model by Lane

### 12.1 VIP Guest Concierge

Default activation:

- operator approval first
- planning deposit optional by operator decision
- payment/deposit required before confirmed service

### 12.2 Team Family Support

Default activation:

- operator approval first
- privacy-sensitive activation allowed
- planning deposit optional
- payment/deposit required before confirmed service

### 12.3 Media / Corporate

Default activation:

- corporate/coordinator approval
- payment pathway or invoice pathway identified
- planning deposit optional
- quote required before fulfillment

### 12.4 Team Fans

Default activation:

- free hub first
- verified preview for custom planning
- planning deposit or activated hub fee for personalized itinerary
- ads/referrals/affiliate paths for non-paying fan traffic
- fulfillment only if matched/confirmed

## 13. Commercial Positioning

Recommended public framing:

`Start with free matchday guidance. Verify your inquiry to build a personalized hub preview. Activate your Personal Matchday Command Hub when you want detailed itinerary planning, operator review, and quote-ready concierge support.`

For VIP/Team Family:

`VIP and Team Family hubs are reviewed by request and may be activated by operator approval before detailed planning or service confirmation.`

For Media/Corporate:

`Media and Corporate command hubs support coordinator-led planning, group movement, catering, recurring schedules, and quote preparation.`

For Fans:

`Fan hubs provide free guidance, with optional activated planning for personalized routes, food, sightseeing, and matchday support.`

## 14. Monetization Gain/Loss Control

Gain occurs when:

- public traffic converts to verified inquiry
- verified inquiry converts to activated hub
- activated hub converts to planning deposit
- planning produces referral/affiliate/API revenue
- planning converts to direct fulfillment
- fan traffic is monetized without operator overload
- VIP/Team/Media/Corporate clients receive premium hub treatment without ads

Loss occurs when:

- personalized itinerary is given away fully for free
- fan traffic consumes VIP/operator time
- third-party referrals are not monetized
- fulfillment is overpromised
- premium hubs are diluted by ads
- coordinators cannot pay or activate
- hub tiers are not clearly separated

## 15. Claim Boundaries

Allowed claims:

- Free matchday guidance available
- Verified hub preview available after secure intake
- Activated hub supports personalized planning
- VIP, Team Family, and Media/Corporate hubs are reviewed by request
- Fulfillment depends on operator confirmation and provider availability
- Some third-party options may be referrals or affiliate links

Prohibited claims:

- activation guarantees service
- payment guarantees VIP approval
- free hub includes full personalized itinerary
- ads/affiliate partners are official tournament partners unless verified
- referral providers are directly controlled unless they are
- third-party fulfillment is guaranteed

## 17. Final Determination

The DFW Command Hub commercial model is accepted as a tiered monetization system:

- public hub captures traffic
- free fan/team hubs scale information delivery
- verified previews qualify serious inquiries
- activated personal hubs monetize planning
- VIP and Team Family hubs protect privacy and scarcity
- Media/Corporate hubs support coordinator-led high-value logistics
- fan hubs may use ads/referrals/affiliate links
- direct fulfillment remains premium but optional

This model preserves monetization when fulfillment capacity is unavailable and prevents the business from depending solely on rides, direct food delivery, or hospitality execution.
