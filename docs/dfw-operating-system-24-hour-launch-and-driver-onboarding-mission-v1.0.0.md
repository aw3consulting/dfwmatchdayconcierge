## 6. Driver Onboarding Lane Purpose

The driver onboarding lane exists to recruit capacity while inquiries begin.

Driver onboarding is not a public employment guarantee.

Driver onboarding is not an automatic approval workflow.

Driver onboarding is an operator-reviewed candidate intake lane for drivers or vehicle operators who may support approved matchday service requests, referrals, overflow, or capacity-controlled direct fulfillment.

## 7. Driver Candidate Intake Requirements

The driver onboarding lane must request or guide candidates to provide:

- name
- phone
- email
- city/base area
- vehicle year, make, model, and color
- seating capacity
- luggage capacity
- insurance readiness
- license readiness
- availability windows
- airport familiarity
- Arlington/stadium familiarity
- Dallas/Fort Worth familiarity
- language capability
- professional driving or hospitality experience
- interest category
- document review readiness

Sensitive data rule:

Driver candidates must not send SSN, bank account details, full ID images, or sensitive personal documents through public email unless explicitly requested through a secure review step.
