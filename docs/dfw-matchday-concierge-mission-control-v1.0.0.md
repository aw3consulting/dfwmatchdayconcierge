## Current Determination
The current PR direction is insufficient because it remains too close to a generic premium transportation or concierge landing page. The correct product is a monetized DFW World Cup Matchday Operating System for arriving and international fans, media, corporate guests, groups, and event support.

## Superseding Product Definition
DFW Matchday Concierge is a concierge booking platform, sports event utility, tourist guide, and matchday operations layer. The site must monetize first through booking widgets, then support conversion through maps, routing, team pages, language selection, food, sightseeing, standings, and concierge intelligence.

## Primary Funnel Split
1. Media / Corporate Hub
2. Local / International Fan Hub

Media and corporate traffic has highest monetization priority because these guests are likely to book through company cards, require scheduling, recurring movement, large group coordination, catering, credential timing, airport-hotel-stadium loops, and dispatch reliability.

Fan traffic requires heavier team identity, language, arrival, sightseeing, FIFA Fan Experience, food, shared ride, and local DFW guidance.

## Operating Reality
The current operator is solo-driver led with 2-3 available delivery personnel and the ability to recruit additional drivers from airport rideshare queues. The build must therefore be a solid shell with controlled dispatch, driver registration, delivery partner registration, inquiry intake, assignment status, and payment request workflow before claiming full live automation.

## Payment Layer Determination
Launch payment flow must remain operator-confirmed:
1. Guest submits request.
2. Operator confirms availability and quote.
3. Payment request or deposit request is sent.
4. Booking is confirmed after payment or approved deposit.
5. Balance and add-ons are settled before pickup, delivery, or completion.

Payment options may include Zelle, Cash App, Venmo, Square payment link, Revolut payment request, card link, or approved cash. International payment options must remain controlled until tested end-to-end.

## Blocked Public Claims
- Official FIFA affiliation
- Guaranteed ticket validity
- Ticket brokerage
- Ticket marketplace
- Escrow
- Fully automated dispatch
- Guaranteed driver supply
- Live payment automation
- Full multi-currency support without tested workflow
