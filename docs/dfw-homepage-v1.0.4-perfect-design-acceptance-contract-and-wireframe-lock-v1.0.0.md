## Purpose

This contract defines the accepted target for the next DFW Matchday Concierge homepage prototype.

It exists because a perfect design cannot be created by broad governance language alone. The homepage target must be defined before code, visual polish, PDF capture, or linked-page mockup work begins.

## Perfect Design Definition

For this homepage, perfect means:

`Minimal premium gateway / fast to understand / visually confident / no repeated content / clear platform introduction / clear audience welcome / clear routing / clear ad opportunity / approved partner recognition / trust and responsibility / details routed to linked pages`

The homepage must feel like a premium public gateway for world-class event visitors, not a directory, dashboard, document, or full guide page.

## Required Homepage Role

The homepage may perform only these six functions:

1. introduce DFW Matchday Concierge
2. qualify the platform as independent and planning-first
3. route visitors to the correct path
4. monetize through premium ad and sponsor opportunity
5. recognize approved partners or available partner space
6. establish trust and responsibility

## Hard Content Limits

The v1.0.4 homepage must follow these limits:

1. maximum primary sections before footer: 6
2. maximum primary CTAs in the hero: 3
3. maximum total homepage copy before footer and disclaimer: 500 words
4. maximum explanation per card: 25 words
5. maximum repeated concept appearances: 2
6. no full guide explanations on the homepage
7. no full sponsor inventory table on the homepage
8. no planning directory behavior
9. no backend or operations language
10. no unsupported live fulfillment claims

## Locked Section Order

The approved v1.0.4 homepage section order is:

1. Header and navigation
2. Hero and welcome
3. What DFW Matchday Concierge provides
4. Choose your path
5. Premium ad space and partner recognition
6. Trust and responsibility
7. Footer and disclaimer

## Wireframe Lock

The v1.0.4 wireframe must use this structure.

### Header

Left: approved logo and DFW Matchday Concierge identity.

Center: concise navigation only.

Right: Advertise With Us CTA.

### Hero and welcome

Large premium hero introducing DFW Matchday Concierge and the Information + Planning Hub.

Required message:

1. welcome teams, supporters, fans, visitors, coordinators, and local partners
2. qualify the event environment without implying official affiliation
3. explain that the platform helps visitors find information, planning paths, and support options

Allowed hero CTAs:

1. Start Planning
2. View Guides
3. Advertise With Us

### What DFW Matchday Concierge provides

Use a compact premium card row. Allowed cards:

1. Visitor Information
2. Matchday Planning
3. Transportation and Arrival Guidance
4. Hotels, Dining, and Area Paths
5. Team Fan and Supporter Routes
6. Reviewed Coordinator Support

No card may contain detailed guide content.

### Choose your path

Use audience path cards. Allowed paths:

1. Fans and Supporters
2. Families and Groups
3. International Visitors
4. VIP, Team, Media, and Corporate Coordinators
5. Advertisers and Local Partners

### Premium ad space and partner recognition

Use one premium commercial band that combines:

1. Title Sponsor Available
2. Guide and Category Sponsorship Available
3. Partner Recognition Spaces Available
4. View Sponsor Opportunities CTA

No fake logos. No sold-looking treatment unless sold and approved.

### Trust and responsibility

Use compact trust statements:

1. Independent Platform
2. Source Distinction
3. Sponsor Disclosure
4. Privacy and Responsible Planning

### Footer and disclaimer

Use concise footer links and compact disclaimer only.
