Independent Service Scope & Public Claims

Independent Planning Surface

DFW Matchday Concierge is an independent information, planning, coordination, and inquiry surface. No official tournament, venue, team, airport, hotel, or government affiliation is claimed.

Confirmed Scope Required

Every request is subject to direct operator review, availability, lawful operating scope, accepted itinerary details, and confirmed service terms before fulfillment.

Provider Responsibility

When third-party service providers are used, each provider remains responsible for its own licensing, insurance, safety standards, pricing, and service performance.

No Automatic Guarantee

Submitting an inquiry does not guarantee availability, pricing, acceptance, route timing, partner availability, or completion until the request is confirmed.
