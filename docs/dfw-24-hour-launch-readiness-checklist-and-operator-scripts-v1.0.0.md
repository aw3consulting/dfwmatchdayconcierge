## 3. First Response Script

Use for inbound clients, coordinators, VIPs, media/corporate, fan, driver, concierge, or fulfillment requests:

`Thank you for reaching DFW Matchday Concierge. Please send your lane, dates, locations, group size, matchday needs, and any timing or luggage details. We will review the request and respond with next steps. Service, pricing, vehicle class, assignments, referrals, and fulfillment are confirmed only after operator review.`

## 4. Coordinator Script

`Please send the group type, arrival and departure dates, airports, hotel area, matchdays, group size, schedule windows, luggage or equipment needs, and budget or payment-readiness status. We will review the plan and prepare the next action path.`

## 5. VIP Script

`Please send arrival date, departure date, airport or FBO, hotel or stay area, party size, luggage count, matchday schedule, privacy needs, comfort requests, and preferred communication method. Black Executive Vehicle Class eligibility and operator assignment are reviewed before confirmation.`

## 6. Media / Corporate Script

`Please send organization name, coordinator contact, group size, executive or crew count, airport, hotel cluster, matchday schedule, luggage or equipment needs, catering or hospitality needs, and quote timing. We will review and respond with a quote-ready next step.`

## 7. Fan Hub Script

`Please send your team, language preference, matchday, stay area, group size, ride interest, food or fan experience interests, and whether you want free guidance or activated planning. Shared ride or fulfillment options require review.`

## 8. Driver Recruiting Script

`Thank you for your driver interest. Please send your name, phone, base area, vehicle year/make/model/color, seat count, luggage fit, airport familiarity, stadium-area familiarity, availability windows, and professional driving or hospitality experience. Driver interest requires operator review and does not guarantee approval or assignments.`

## 9. Concierge Recruiting Script

`Thank you for your concierge interest. Please send your name, phone, location, availability, hospitality or operations experience, language skills, coordination experience, and preferred support area. Concierge interest requires operator review and does not guarantee approval or assignments.`

## 10. Fulfillment Script

`Please send the confirmed need, lane, date, time, origin, destination, group size, driver need, concierge need, referral need, urgency, and any issue or closeout details. Fulfillment begins only after scope and operator-approved routing are confirmed.`

## 11. Final Determination

These scripts and checklist support manual inquiry readiness only.

Production remains blocked until all review gates pass and the user explicitly approves deployment.
