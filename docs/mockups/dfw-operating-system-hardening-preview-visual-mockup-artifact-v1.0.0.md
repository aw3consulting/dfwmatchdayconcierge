## 6. Governed Design Decisions Captured

The artifact captures the following decisions:

- DFW Matchday Concierge is presented as a World Cup Matchday Operating System, not a ride page.
- Four public lanes are separated above the homepage funnel.
- Inquiry remains primary before service confirmation.
- AI intake is presented as planning preview only.
- Verified hub preview remains separate from activated hub access.
- Activated hub planning remains separate from confirmed service.
- VIP and Tahoe are controlled by operator review, quote, payment/deposit approval, and availability confirmation.
- Team Family privacy and coordinator handling are visually separated.
- Media / Corporate is quote-ready and operational.
- Team Fan Hubs support free guidance plus activated planning.
- Fan monetization is limited to controlled public fan contexts.
- Status badges govern claims, payments, review, and confirmation states.
- Mobile UX prioritizes inquiry and status visibility.
