# DFW Executive Vehicle Class Standard v1.0.0

## 1. Purpose

This file establishes the system-wide vehicle-language correction for DFW Matchday Concierge.

The prior exclusive public-facing vehicle standard:

`Black 2026 Chevrolet Tahoe`

is retired as the system-wide standard.

The new governed standard is:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class`

## 2. Immediate Determination

All public, internal, mockup, UX, intake, profile, command hub, driver, concierge, fulfillment, and governance references must move from a single-vehicle standard to an executive-class vehicle standard.

The corrected public-facing standard is:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class for approved VIP and executive matchday bookings.`

Allowed supporting explanation:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class may include Black Executive SUVs and other eligible executive or presidential-style vehicles, subject to operator review, availability, passenger fit, luggage fit, route needs, comfort requirements, and quote confirmation.`

## 3. Why This Correction Is Required

The prior Tahoe-exclusive language was too narrow for the actual premium operating model.

The operating system must support:

- VIP guests
- team families
- executives
- sponsors
- media coordinators
- corporate groups
- high-profile guests
- group movement
- luggage-sensitive travel
- presidential-style vehicle expectations
- executive and premium SUV availability variations

A single vehicle make/model claim restricts capacity, creates unnecessary expectation risk, and weakens the premium vehicle offering.

## 4. Approved Vehicle Language

Approved public language:

- `Black Executive Vehicle Class`
- `Black Executive SUV`
- `Executive Vehicle Class`
- `Executive or presidential-style vehicle options`
- `Premium black vehicle assignment by review`
- `Vehicle class confirmed after operator review`
- `Vehicle assignment subject to availability, route, capacity, luggage, and comfort fit`

## 5. Restricted Vehicle Language

Restricted after this update:

- `Black 2026 Chevrolet Tahoe` as the universal VIP standard
- `2026 Chevrolet Tahoe` as the default vehicle standard
- `Tahoe eligibility` as the primary public vehicle claim
- `Tahoe allocation` as the system-wide allocation model
- `guaranteed Tahoe`
- `official Tahoe vehicle`
- any public language implying one specific vehicle is guaranteed before confirmation

A specific vehicle may be disclosed only after confirmation or inside a controlled booking-specific context.

## 6. Vehicle Class Scope

Eligible vehicle classes may include:

- Black Executive SUV
- Black Luxury SUV
- presidential-style SUV
- premium executive sedan
- premium executive van
- sprinter-style executive vehicle for group or luggage fit
- other operator-approved executive-class vehicle

Vehicle assignment must remain controlled by:

- operator review
- booking scope
- confirmed quote
- payment or deposit status where applicable
- passenger count
- luggage count
- route and timing requirements
- comfort expectations
- availability
- service quality standards

## 7. Required Website Update

All website pages must replace Tahoe-exclusive language with Black Executive Vehicle Class language.

Required examples:

Replace:

`Black 2026 Chevrolet Tahoe eligibility for approved bookings.`

With:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class eligibility for approved VIP and executive bookings.`

Replace:

`Tahoe and operator assignment require review.`

With:

`Vehicle class and operator assignment require review.`

Replace:

`VIP Vehicle Standard: Black 2026 Chevrolet Tahoe`

With:

`VIP Vehicle Standard: Black Executive Vehicle Class`

## 8. Required Mockup Update

All future mockups must visually represent the vehicle layer as a premium black executive vehicle class, not as one specific model.

Visual direction:

- premium black executive SUV
- executive black vehicle lineup
- presidential-style vehicle tone
- no brand-specific guarantee unless booking-specific
- no fake official vehicle endorsement

## 9. Claim Boundary

Allowed boundary language:

`Executive vehicle assignment is confirmed after operator review, quote approval, payment or deposit status where applicable, availability, passenger fit, luggage fit, route needs, and comfort requirements.`

Required disclaimer:

`No specific vehicle make or model is guaranteed before confirmation.`

## 10. Final Determination

The DFW Matchday Concierge vehicle standard is now:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class`

This correction applies system-wide immediately.

All future execution, copy, mockups, frontend pages, intake screens, profile pages, command hubs, data files, and governance records must use this standard unless the user explicitly approves a booking-specific vehicle reference.
