# DFW VIP Guest Concierge Governance Layer v1.0.1

## 1. Purpose

This file governs the VIP Guest Concierge lane for DFW Matchday Concierge. It defines eligibility, allocation, booking minimums, Black Executive Vehicle Class access, primary operator priority, Team Family escalation, Media / Corporate escalation, payment, cancellation, anonymity, and public claim boundaries.

This layer controls the highest-value and highest-sensitivity service lane before any homepage, mockup, booking widget, command hub, profile page, intake page, or public copy presents VIP service.

## 2. Governing Position

VIP Guest Concierge is the highest-priority client lane.

VIP is not a single-trip ride product. VIP is a scheduled, high-touch concierge allocation for weekly, full-stay, multi-day, or approved high-value booking windows.

VIP Guest Concierge must protect scarce resources:

- primary operator time
- Black Executive Vehicle Class availability
- private scheduling attention
- concierge planning bandwidth
- anonymity and privacy handling
- emergency schedule adjustment capacity

## 3. Active Priority Order

The active priority order is:

1. VIP Guest Concierge
2. Team Family Members
3. Media / Corporate
4. Team Fans

VIP remains highest because it receives first review for primary operator availability and Black Executive Vehicle Class allocation.

## 4. Official VIP Vehicle Standard

The official VIP vehicle standard is:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class`

Class definition:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class may include Black Executive SUVs and other eligible executive or presidential-style vehicles, subject to operator review, availability, capacity fit, route requirements, comfort requirements, luggage needs, and quote confirmation.`

Public reference may describe this as:

`Black Executive Vehicle Class for approved VIP and executive matchday bookings.`

Do not claim a guaranteed specific make, model, vehicle, driver, or vehicle class before booking confirmation.

## 5. VIP Service Format

### 5.1 Allowed VIP Formats

VIP Guest Concierge may be offered as:

- full-stay booking
- weekly booking
- multi-day booking
- approved full-day block
- approved high-value day block connected to credential/access priority

### 5.2 Prohibited VIP Formats

VIP Guest Concierge must not be offered as:

- single-trip VIP ride
- shared VIP ride
- pool-driver VIP ride
- public instant VIP upgrade
- unqualified fan VIP transport
- guaranteed 24/7 immediate pickup without scheduling

## 6. 24/7 Scheduled Concierge Rule

VIP may be framed as around-the-clock scheduled concierge support during the confirmed service window.

Allowed language:

`24/7 scheduled VIP concierge support during the confirmed booking window.`

`Around-the-clock coordination by approved schedule.`

`Dedicated planning and movement support across the confirmed stay.`

Prohibited language:

- unlimited instant service
- immediate pickup guaranteed at all times
- unlimited unscheduled transport
- always-available private fleet
- guaranteed event access

## 7. VIP Eligibility Scoring

VIP inquiries should be scored before acceptance.

### 7.1 Scoring Factors

Recommended scoring factors:

- booking duration
- expected booking value
- credential/access relevance
- team/federation relationship
- sponsor/hospitality relationship
- Team Family status
- media/corporate profile
- privacy sensitivity
- passenger count
- itinerary complexity
- payment readiness
- referral quality
- schedule feasibility
- Black Executive Vehicle Class availability
- operator availability

### 7.2 Priority Signals

High-priority signals:

- weekly or full-stay request
- confirmed multi-day movement need
- Team Family escalation
- credentialed guest or guest of credential holder
- sponsor or hospitality guest
- federation-linked guest
- high-profile traveler
- high-value bid
- concierge needs beyond transportation
- payment-ready client

### 7.3 Low-Priority or Decline Signals

Low-priority signals:

- single ride only
- price-shopping behavior
- no schedule clarity
- no deposit readiness
- fan shared-ride request disguised as VIP
- request requiring official access claim
- unrealistic 24/7 immediate-service expectation
- public fan route better suited for pool-driver lane

## 8. Credential and Access Handling

Credential/access status is a prioritization input, not a verification promise.

Allowed intake values:

- credentialed
- pending credential
- guest of credential holder
- sponsor guest
- federation guest
- Team Family
- Media / Corporate
- other private guest

Public claim restriction:

DFW Matchday Concierge must not claim official credential validation, official tournament status, official event access, or official team authorization.

Recommended language:

`Credential or access status may help prioritize scheduling and service planning. DFW Matchday Concierge does not provide official credential validation or event access authorization.`

## 9. Primary Operator Allocation

The primary operator is a scarce resource.

Allocation order:

1. confirmed VIP Guest Concierge
2. Team Family escalation into VIP
3. high-profile Media / Corporate escalation
4. highest-value approved private booking
5. strategic credential/access opportunity
6. exceptional fan upgrade only by approval

The primary operator should not be allocated to ordinary fan shared rides, general pool-driver work, or unqualified single-trip bookings when VIP demand exists.

## 10. Black Executive Vehicle Class Allocation Logic

The Black Executive Vehicle Class should be allocated in this order:

1. confirmed VIP Guest Concierge full-stay / weekly client
2. confirmed VIP Guest Concierge multi-day client
3. Team Family VIP escalation
4. Media / Corporate high-profile or high-value booking
5. approved private day block
6. approved premium upgrade only when schedule permits

If Black Executive Vehicle Class availability is blocked, the inquiry may be:

- quoted with alternate vehicle disclosure
- waitlisted
- downgraded to private non-VIP service
- declined
- referred to trusted driver pool where appropriate

No public page may imply any specific executive vehicle is guaranteed before confirmation.

## 11. Team Family Escalation Rules

Team Family Members may escalate into VIP Guest Concierge when any of the following apply:

- weekly or full-stay support
- multi-day family support
- privacy or anonymity request
- high-profile or player-adjacent sensitivity
- direct player / staff / federation family context
- airport + hotel + stadium + food/shopping/sightseeing needs
- limited family group requiring primary operator discretion
- payment-ready high-value request

Team Family Members may also remain in Team Family Support without full VIP escalation when their request is lower-touch or can be supported through trusted operators.

## 12. Media / Corporate Escalation Rules

Media / Corporate may escalate into VIP Guest Concierge when:

- executive or sponsor guest requires private movement
- credentialed guest requires primary operator discretion
- booking is high-value or multi-day
- recurring movement requires executive vehicle class priority
- company-card or invoice value justifies allocation
- profile or operational sensitivity warrants private treatment

Media / Corporate should remain in its own lane when:

- group movement can be assigned to partner drivers
- catering or delivery is primary need
- crew transport is operational rather than privacy-sensitive
- multi-vehicle dispatch is more important than one premium vehicle allocation

## 13. Team Fan Exclusion Rule

Team Fans are excluded from VIP Transport by default.

Team Fans may use:

- shared rides
- pool-driver routing
- group rides
- fan packages
- food and drink support
- Fan Experience routes
- Team Matchday Hubs

Team Fans may escalate only when:

- high-value private request is approved
- weekly/full-stay support is requested
- credential/access relevance exists
- Black Executive Vehicle Class and operator capacity exist
- payment and deposit conditions are satisfied

## 14. Anonymity and Privacy Protocol

VIP Guests and Team Family Members have the right to remain anonymous in public-facing and operationally unnecessary contexts.

Collected for service coordination:

- first name
- last name
- phone
- email
- hotel / pickup location
- service dates
- party size
- luggage/equipment needs
- concierge requests
- payment contact

Not publicly required:

- identity confirmation
- relationship proof
- celebrity/player connection disclosure
- credential upload
- public verification

Operational handling:

- use booking code internally when appropriate
- use initials or first name only for assigned driver notes when possible
- keep full names limited to primary operator and payment/coordination need
- avoid public or promotional reference to clients without explicit permission

## 16. Deposit and Payment Governance

VIP bookings require payment discipline before scarce capacity is held.

### 16.1 VIP Deposit Rule

VIP Guest Concierge requires a deposit before confirmation.

Suggested structure until final pricing is set:

- inquiry: no charge
- quote: operator-confirmed
- hold: deposit required
- confirmation: deposit accepted and service window confirmed
- add-ons: prepaid or approved reimbursement
- balance: due before or during confirmed service window as defined in quote

### 16.2 Weekly / Full-Stay Bookings

Weekly or full-stay bookings require:

- quote
- deposit
- written service window
- itinerary assumptions
- cancellation terms
- add-on/reimbursement rules

### 16.3 Payment Methods

Potential accepted methods:

- card payment link
- Zelle
- Cash App
- Venmo
- Revolut request by approval
- company-card or invoice for approved Media / Corporate escalation
- approved cash only by exception

Public language:

`Payment method is confirmed after quote and availability review.`

## 17. Cancellation and Refund Shell

VIP cancellation and refund handling depends on timing, allocation, expenses, and assignment.

Recommended shell:

- Full refund before operator, vehicle class, or service window is held.
- Partial refund after operator, vehicle class, or service window is reserved.
- Purchased food, supplies, event-day expenses, delivery items, and custom requests are non-refundable once purchased.
- Late cancellation, no-show, or schedule abandonment may forfeit deposit.
- Weather, traffic, event security, road closure, official restriction, or flight disruption reviewed case by case.

## 18. Bidding and Priority Logic

When multiple qualified VIP, Team Family, or Media / Corporate requests compete for the same service window, allocation should consider:

- confirmed payment
- booking value
- duration
- privacy sensitivity
- credential/access relevance
- referral quality
- itinerary complexity
- executive vehicle class/operator availability
- strategic future value

Highest-profile or highest-bid clients may receive priority only when service quality and claim boundaries remain controlled.

## 19. Public Copy Boundaries

Allowed public claims:

- VIP Guest Concierge by request

- Black Executive Vehicle Class for approved VIP and executive matchday bookings
- Team Family Support by request
- private scheduled concierge support

VIP Guest Concierge is accepted as a scarce-capacity, high-value, privacy-sensitive concierge lane.

The lane is governed by:

- multi-day / weekly / full-stay preference
- no single-trip VIP public product
- no shared VIP rides
- Black Executive Vehicle Class priority
- primary operator priority
