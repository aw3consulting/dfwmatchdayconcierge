## 4. Strategic Determination

DFW Matchday Concierge must treat its public site as a commercial information and planning gateway, not a transportation-only page.

The scalable commercial asset is the ability to organize high-intent World Cup visitor demand across:

- arrival planning
- airport guidance
- matchday movement
- fan routing
- restaurant and bar discovery
- watch-party discovery
- hotel-area orientation
- attraction planning
- group and coordinator intake
- sponsor-safe public recommendations
- affiliate and referral conversion
- activated command hub conversion
- premium concierge lead capture

Direct fulfillment remains premium and capacity-controlled. Public information, planning, routing, sponsored visibility, referral conversion, and activated hub conversion are the scalable monetization layer.

## 5. Market Basis for Commercial Expansion

Dallas is a high-value World Cup market because Dallas Stadium at AT&T Stadium is scheduled for nine World Cup matches, including five group-stage matches, two Round of 32 matches, one Round of 16 match, and one semifinal.

Public research also identifies Dallas as the International Broadcast Center location for the 2026 FIFA World Cup at the Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center, creating a meaningful media, production, sponsor, and coordinator opportunity.

External research references used for this package:

- Guardian 2026 World Cup stadium guide, June 1, 2026
- Houston Chronicle reporting on Dallas International Broadcast Center, March 6, 2025
- AS reporting on Dallas International Broadcast Center and expected journalist volume, March 5, 2025
- FIFA and public sponsor reporting sources reviewed during package creation

All external research must remain verification input, not public claim authority, until converted into approved public copy through claim review.

## 9. Ad Product Stack

### 9.1 Founding Sponsorship

Purpose:
Top-level commercial authority surface for early sponsor outreach.

Placement:
Homepage, public gateway, partner page, selected public guide headers.

Ad status:
Sponsored Placement.

Pricing posture:
Highest ask, limited availability.

### 9.2 Category Sponsorship

Purpose:
Own one vertical guide.

Placement examples:

- Airport Arrival Planning Partner
- Restaurant and Watch-Party Partner
- Media / Corporate Planning Partner
- Team Fan Hub Partner
- Family Visitor Guide Partner

Ad status:
Sponsored Placement or Curated Partner Placement.

### 9.3 Team Fan Hub Sponsorship

Purpose:
Convert high-volume team and country fan traffic.

Placement:
Team hub header, matchday checklist, restaurant/watch-party module, route guide module, affiliate cards.

Ad status:
Sponsored Placement with Information Only or Third-Party Referral badges as needed.

### 9.4 Featured Guide Card

Purpose:
Scalable local business visibility.

Placement:
Area guides, route guides, food guides, attraction guides, public fan hubs.

Ad status:
Sponsored Placement or Affiliate / Referral Link.

### 9.5 Affiliate and Referral Routing

Purpose:
Monetize non-fulfilled demand.

Eligible pathways:

- hotel referral
- restaurant reservation
- food delivery
- attraction tickets
- parking
- luggage storage
- eSIM
- ride platform deep link
- shuttle or private transport referral
- travel support

Ad status:
Affiliate / Referral Link or Third-Party Provider.

### 9.6 Curated Premium Resource Placement

Purpose:
Provide high-trust resources in or near premium contexts without making the experience feel ad-supported.

Allowed only when expressly approved.

Eligible contexts:

- public VIP information page
- public Media / Corporate information page
- public Team Family information page
- operator-reviewed resource directory

Blocked contexts unless expressly approved:

- paid VIP hub
- Team Family private hub
- Media / Corporate private hub
- privacy-sensitive activated hub

## 15. Final Determination

The ad lane is now expanded into a full Marketing Strategy and Monetization Blueprint.

The highest-value path is to sell Founding Sponsorship, Semifinal Week Premium Takeover, Category Sponsorship, Team Fan Hub Sponsorship, Route / Area Guide Sponsorship, Featured Partner Cards, Affiliate / Referral Routing, and Group Lead Packages in that order.

Public fan and information surfaces may carry monetized placements. VIP, Team Family, Media / Corporate private hubs, activated premium hubs, and privacy-sensitive hubs remain protected from standard display advertising.
