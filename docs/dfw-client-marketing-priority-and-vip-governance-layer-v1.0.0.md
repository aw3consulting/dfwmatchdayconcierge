# DFW Client Marketing Priority and VIP Governance Layer v1.0.0

## 1. Purpose

This file hardens the accepted DFW Matchday Concierge business model for public funnel design, client marketing priority, fulfillment allocation, VIP eligibility, Team Family Members handling, anonymity, and claim control.

This layer governs the next mockup, website architecture, booking widgets, intake forms, client segmentation, dispatch logic, and service allocation.

## 2. Active Product Definition

DFW Matchday Concierge is a World Cup Matchday Concierge operating system for credential-sensitive, privacy-sensitive, arriving, international, media, corporate, team-family, VIP, and fan traffic in Dallas / Fort Worth.

The platform must behave as a concierge intake system. It may accept broad inquiries, but all inquiries must be routed, quoted, prioritized, assigned, restricted, downgraded, escalated, or declined through controlled logic.

## 3. Client Marketing Priority Determination

The accepted priority order is:

1. VIP Guest Concierge
2. Team Family Members
3. Media / Corporate
4. Team Fans

This updates the previous order because Team Family Members may sit closer to celebrity / access-sensitive movement than standard media or corporate guests. Team Family Members may require privacy, anonymity, dedicated handling, and limited per-matchday capacity. Their demand profile may be lower volume than Media / Corporate but higher sensitivity and closer to VIP service logic.

## 4. Priority Rationale

### 4.1 VIP Guest Concierge

VIP Guest Concierge remains the highest-priority commercial and operational lane.

VIP is not a single ride product. VIP is a multi-day, weekly, or entire-stay concierge allocation.

VIP may include:

- credential-linked guests
- access-linked guests
- sponsor-linked guests
- federation-linked guests
- high-profile travelers
- ultra-high-spend travelers
- celebrity-adjacent guests
- approved private referrals
- team-family escalations
- media/corporate escalations

### 4.2 Team Family Members

Team Family Members are positioned above Media / Corporate for priority allocation because they may require:

- anonymous handling
- privacy-sensitive movement
- limited-availability personal support
- family group coordination
- airport-heavy movement
- hotel-heavy movement
- matchday timing protection
- shopping, food, and sightseeing support
- multi-day support
- protection from public fan routing
- elevated operator discretion

Team Family Members may be limited to one or two accepted clients per matchday, multi-day block, or weekly block depending on VIP allocation and driver capacity.

### 4.3 Media / Corporate

Media / Corporate remains a high-value monetization lane with strong company-card and recurring-route potential, but it can be fulfilled through a wider operator / driver / delivery network than VIP or Team Family service.

Media / Corporate may include:

- journalists
- production crews
- sponsors
- corporate guests
- hospitality teams
- executives
- officials
- recurring route clients
- catering clients
- equipment / luggage movement clients

### 4.4 Team Fans

Team Fans remain a volume lane and must be kept separate from VIP Transport.

Team Fans are eligible for:

- shared rides
- pool-driver fulfillment
- route packages
- fan experience trips
- food / snack / drink support
- group movement
- luggage coordination
- team hub booking

Team Fans are not eligible for VIP Transport unless approved for escalation into VIP Guest Concierge.

## 5. VIP Guest Concierge Governance

### 5.1 VIP Minimum Booking Rule

VIP Guest Concierge must not be sold as a single-trip service.

Allowed VIP formats:

- weekly booking
- full-stay booking
- multi-day booking
- approved daily block only when strategically justified

Prohibited VIP formats:

- single-trip VIP ride
- VIP shared ride
- public fan VIP upgrade without approval
- pool-driver VIP fulfillment

### 5.2 Official VIP Vehicle Allocation

The official Matchday Concierge VIP vehicle is:

- 2026 Chevrolet Tahoe
- black
- VIP configuration / presentation

The Tahoe must be allocated only to:

- VIP Guest Concierge
- approved Team Family Members
- approved Media / Corporate high-priority movement
- operator-discretion assignments

Team Fans must not be shown or promised Tahoe VIP transport unless they are explicitly escalated and approved.

### 5.3 Operator Allocation Rule

The primary operator is limited and must be reserved for the highest-value and highest-sensitivity clients.

Primary operator priority:

1. confirmed VIP Guest Concierge
2. approved Team Family Members
3. high-profile Media / Corporate
4. highest-bidder or strategic credential/access opportunity
5. overflow only by approval

### 5.4 24/7 VIP Concierge Language

VIP Concierge may be described as around-the-clock scheduled concierge support, but not as uncontrolled immediate service availability.

Allowed framing:

`24/7 scheduled VIP concierge support during the confirmed booking window.`

`Around-the-clock planning and coordination by approved schedule.`

Avoid:

- instant unlimited availability
- guaranteed immediate pickup at all times
- unlimited unscheduled service
- full fleet claim
- guaranteed official access

## 6. Credential and Access Eligibility

Credential/access eligibility is a prioritization signal, not a public verification promise.

Inputs may include:

- media credential status
- team credential status
- sponsor guest status
- federation guest status
- player family relationship
- team staff family relationship
- hospitality access
- event schedule relevance
- referral source
- expected booking value

Allowed intake language:

- credential/access status
- credentialed
- pending credential
- guest of credential holder
- sponsor guest
- team family request
- media/corporate request

Prohibited public claim language:

- verified by DFW Matchday Concierge
- official credential validation
- official tournament access
- guaranteed stadium access
- official FIFA partner
- ticket authentication
- team-approved transport

## 7. Anonymity and Privacy Rule

VIP and Team Family Members have the right to remain anonymous in public-facing and operationally unnecessary contexts.

Operationally collected:

- first name
- last name
- phone
- email
- hotel or pickup location
- service schedule
- guest count
- luggage/equipment details
- concierge needs

Not required publicly:

- relationship proof
- credential image upload
- public identity confirmation
- celebrity or player association disclosure

Internal handling rule:

Use first and last names only for direct service coordination. Public or dispatch-facing references may use initials, booking code, lane type, or first name only when appropriate.

## 8. Team Family Member Layer Hardening

Team Family Members should have a distinct lane, but it should not overexpose them publicly.

Recommended public label:

`Team Family Support`

Alternative public labels:

- Team Guest Support
- Family & Team Guest Support
- Private Team Guest Concierge

Avoid:

- player family verification
- celebrity family language
- official team family claim
- guaranteed team affiliation

### 8.1 Team Family Capacity Rule

Team Family Members may be accepted selectively.

Suggested capacity:

- one to two Team Family clients per matchday when VIP lane is open
- one weekly Team Family client if primary operator availability is limited
- more only when trusted driver / delivery support is available

### 8.2 Team Family Escalation to VIP

Team Family requests escalate to VIP Guest Concierge when they include:

- full-stay support
- weekly support
- privacy requirement
- high profile or celebrity-adjacent status
- direct player/federation connection
- multiple family members
- airport + hotel + stadium + shopping/food/sightseeing schedule
- high-value bid

## 9. Media / Corporate Layer Review

Media / Corporate is commercially strong and should remain highly visible, but it should not automatically outrank Team Family Members for primary operator allocation.

Media / Corporate advantages:

- company-card payment
- group bookings
- catering
- recurring schedules
- multi-vehicle potential
- less anonymity sensitivity in many cases
- greater pool-driver compatibility

Media / Corporate limitations:

- may be operationally broader but less personally sensitive
- can often be fulfilled by assigned drivers or partner operators
- should receive Tahoe / primary operator only when profile, payment, route, or credential value justifies it

## 10. Team Fan Layer Restriction

Team Fans are the public volume funnel.

Team Fans are excluded from VIP Transport by default.

Team Fan fulfillment should use:

- pool drivers
- shared ride logic
- group ride matching
- route packages
- fan hub booking
- food / snack / cooler support
- operator-assisted routing

Team Fans may be escalated only when:

- full-stay or weekly spend is high
- private booking value exceeds other open priority lanes
- credential/access relevance exists
- operator approves the upgrade

## 11. Public Homepage Lane Order

Recommended public lane order:

1. VIP Guest Concierge
2. Team Family Support
3. Media / Corporate
4. Team Fan Hubs

If the homepage needs a broader first split, use:

- Private / VIP
- Media / Corporate
- Team Family
- Fans

VIP and Team Family should feel private and selective. Media / Corporate should feel operational and schedulable. Team Fans should feel visual, team-based, social, shared-ride eligible, and tourism-oriented.

## 12. Booking Widget Priority Logic

The booking widget must first identify the lane:

- VIP Guest Concierge
- Team Family Support
- Media / Corporate
- Team Fan

Then collect:

- length of stay / service window
- matchday dates
- credential/access status if applicable
- team/federation/fan context if applicable
- pickup and hotel details
- passengers
- luggage/equipment
- food/catering/support needs
- privacy preference
- payment readiness
- notes

## 13. Deposit and Payment Direction

VIP Guest Concierge:

- deposit required
- weekly/full-stay quote required
- balance timing controlled by operator
- no confirmation until payment/deposit accepted

Team Family Members:

- deposit required
- may use VIP-style quote when privacy or full-stay needs apply
- payment must be confirmed before allocation of scarce primary operator/Tahoe capacity

Media / Corporate:

- quote required
- deposit or company-card payment link required
- recurring or multi-vehicle bookings require stronger payment confirmation

Team Fans:

- prepayment or deposit required for private rides/packages
- shared ride requests remain operator-matched and not guaranteed until confirmed

## 14. Claim Control

Allowed public claims:

- VIP Guest Concierge by request
- Team Family Support by request
- Media / Corporate movement support
- Team Fan Hubs
- Shared ride requests for fans
- Operator-confirmed booking
- 2026 Black Chevrolet Tahoe for approved VIP service
- Pool-driver fan transport where assigned

Restricted or prohibited claims:

- guaranteed VIP availability
- guaranteed Tahoe availability
- guaranteed official access
- verified team family status
- official media credential validation
- official FIFA affiliation
- automatic shared ride matching
- fan access to VIP Transport by default
- unlimited 24/7 immediate service

## 15. Hardening Determination

The hardened business model is accepted as:

1. VIP Guest Concierge
2. Team Family Members
3. Media / Corporate
4. Team Fans

This order is for primary marketing priority, scarcity allocation, operator attention, and website funnel emphasis.

Media / Corporate remains a major monetization lane and may outrank Team Family Members in volume and company-card revenue. However, Team Family Members outrank Media / Corporate in privacy sensitivity, relationship proximity, celebrity-adjacent treatment, and potential VIP escalation.
