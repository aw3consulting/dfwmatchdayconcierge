# DFW Ad Inventory and Sponsored Placement Governance v1.0.0

## 1. Purpose

This file governs ad inventory, sponsored placements, affiliate placements, referral cards, curated partner modules, and premium resource placements across the DFW Matchday Concierge Information + Planning Hub.

The purpose is to monetize high-volume public information traffic while preserving premium trust, privacy boundaries, operator-confirmed fulfillment controls, and unsupported-claim restrictions.

## 2. Controlled Scope

This governance applies to:

- public homepage placements
- public information gateway placements
- free team fan hub placements
- route and area guide placements
- restaurant and watch-party placements
- attraction and experience placements
- affiliate and referral placements
- public Media / Corporate information page placements
- public VIP and Team Family information page placements
- partner and advertise page placements
- future mockup revision surfaces

This governance does not authorize:

- ads inside paid VIP hubs
- ads inside Team Family private hubs
- ads inside Media / Corporate private hubs
- ads inside activated premium command hubs
- ads inside privacy-sensitive hubs
- unverified official sponsor claims
- unverified host city partner claims
- unverified tournament affiliation claims
- fulfillment guarantees

## 3. Inventory Classes

### 3.1 Class A: Founding Premium Inventory

Highest-value inventory for sponsor outreach.

Examples:

- Founding Presenting Partner
- Semifinal Week Premium Takeover
- Official Arrival Planning Partner, only when agreement language is verified
- Media + Corporate Planning Partner
- Team Fan Hub Presenting Sponsor

Required controls:

- written approval before publication
- claim review
- start and end dates
- placement inventory record
- sponsor-safe disclosure
- no official tournament implication unless verified

### 3.2 Class B: High-Volume Public Inventory

Scalable public placements that may support fan traffic.

Examples:

- team fan hub sponsor card
- matchday guide sponsor card
- airport-to-stadium route card
- hotel-zone guide card
- Fair Park guide card
- Arlington guide card
- Dallas guide card
- Fort Worth guide card
- restaurant or watch-party card
- before-kickoff checklist card
- after-match guide card
- between-matches attraction card

Required controls:

- status badge
- disclosure text
- placement category
- expiration date
- blocked-claim review

### 3.3 Class C: Affiliate and Referral Inventory

Monetized routing inventory for third-party offers.

Examples:

- hotel booking referral
- restaurant reservation referral
- attraction ticket referral
- parking referral
- luggage storage referral
- eSIM referral
- rideshare deep link
- food delivery referral
- shuttle or private transport referral

Required controls:

- provider terms reviewed
- commission or bounty model recorded
- data privacy requirements reviewed
- user disclosure added
- geography verified
- third-party fulfillment clearly identified

### 3.4 Class D: Curated Premium Resource Placement

High-trust placement that may appear near premium information surfaces without becoming display advertising.

Eligible only after express approval.

Examples:

- VIP recommended partner directory
- public Team Family resource directory
- public Media / Corporate resource directory
- sponsor/corporate utility card
- operator-reviewed local provider resource

Required controls:

- premium relevance
- curated presentation
- no intrusive ad treatment
- explicit status badge
- no implication of DFW-controlled fulfillment unless directly accepted

## 4. Placement Location Rules

### 4.1 Allowed Public Placement Locations

Ads, sponsored placements, referral cards, and affiliate cards may appear in:

- homepage public sponsor rail
- public information gateway
- free team fan hubs
- public matchday guides
- route and area guides
- restaurant and watch-party guides
- attraction guides
- public fan command hub previews
- partner and advertise page

### 4.2 Restricted Placement Locations

Standard ads must not appear in:

- paid VIP hub
- Team Family private hub
- Media / Corporate private hub
- activated premium command hub
- privacy-sensitive hub
- quote review surface
- payment status surface
- private itinerary surface

Exception:
A curated premium resource may appear only when expressly approved and contextually aligned.

## 5. Required Status Badges

Every monetized placement must include at least one status:

- Sponsored Placement
- Affiliate / Referral Link
- Third-Party Provider
- Official Source
- Information Only
- Operator-Reviewed
- Availability Required
- Quote Required
- Confirmed
- Restricted

No monetized card may appear without a status.

## 6. Required Disclosure Types

### 6.1 Sponsored Placement Disclosure

Allowed disclosure:

`Sponsored placement. DFW Matchday Concierge may receive compensation for this placement. Availability, pricing, and fulfillment are controlled by the listed provider unless separately confirmed by DFW Matchday Concierge.`

### 6.2 Affiliate / Referral Disclosure

Allowed disclosure:

`Affiliate or referral link. DFW Matchday Concierge may receive compensation if you use this link. Third-party terms, availability, pricing, and fulfillment apply.`

### 6.3 Third-Party Provider Disclosure

Allowed disclosure:

`Third-party provider. This option is listed for planning convenience. Final service terms, availability, pricing, and fulfillment are controlled by the provider unless DFW Matchday Concierge separately confirms direct service responsibility.`

### 6.4 Official Source Disclosure

Allowed disclosure:

`Official source link. Use this source for official event, venue, schedule, or policy information.`

## 7. Blocked Claims

Sponsored, affiliate, referral, or partner placements must not claim:

- official FIFA partner
- official tournament partner
- official World Cup partner
- official host city partner
- approved by FIFA
- approved by the host city
- official team partner
- guaranteed ride
- guaranteed driver
- guaranteed security
- guaranteed availability
- guaranteed shared ride
- direct DFW fulfillment unless confirmed

## 8. Approval Workflow

Before publication, each placement must pass:

1. partner identity review
2. category review
3. claim review
4. disclosure review
5. placement location review
6. date and duration review
7. compensation or referral model review
8. privacy and data review where applicable
9. visual treatment review
10. final operator approval

## 9. Data Requirements

Each ad inventory entry must include:

- id
- inventory_class
- placement_name
- placement_type
- allowed_surfaces
- blocked_surfaces
- target_categories
- status_badges
- disclosure_required
- claim_review_required
- approval_status
- start_date
- end_date
- pricing_model
- direct_fulfillment_claim_allowed
- official_affiliation_claim_allowed

## 10. Final Determination

DFW Matchday Concierge may scale public ad, sponsorship, referral, and affiliate revenue through public information surfaces and fan hubs while preserving premium private hubs as trust-first, ad-controlled environments.

All monetized placements require status badges, disclosure language, claim review, and placement location controls before publication.
